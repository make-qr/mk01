What is Monkey Mart?

Monkey Mart is a casual management game by Tiny Dobbins. You start with a single banana stall and grow into a multi-aisle supermarket. The gameplay loop is simple: harvest produce, place it on shelves, collect payments, buy upgrades. But the steady progression — new products, helpers, store floors — makes it hard to stop playing.
