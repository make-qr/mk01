Monkey Mart vs Monkey Mart 2: Key Differences

Should you play the original Monkey Mart or jump straight to Monkey Mart 2? Here is how both versions compare.

Monkey Mart and Monkey Mart 2 are both browser-based supermarket management games by Tiny Dobbins. They share the same core loop — harvest, stock, sell, upgrade — but Monkey Mart 2 expands the formula with more content and features.

What Stays the Same

  • You play as a monkey running a grocery store
  • Harvest crops, stock shelves, serve customers, collect coins
  • Browser-based, no download required
  • Simple controls: WASD or touch
  • Helper monkeys automate tasks

What Monkey Mart 2 Adds

  • More products and production chains
  • Additional store areas and expansion options
  • More helper monkey roles and automation depth
  • Updated visuals and busier late-game traffic
  • Extended progression for long-term players

Which Should You Play First?

New players: Start with the original Monkey Mart on our homepage. The simpler early game teaches harvesting, stocking, and upgrade timing without overwhelming you.

Returning players: If you have mastered the original, Monkey Mart 2 offers more content and late-game depth. Access it from the homepage keyword bar.

Performance and Access

Both versions run in HTML5 browsers on desktop, Chromebook, and mobile. Neither requires an app install — ideal for unblocked play at school or work where downloads are blocked.

Tips Transfer Between Versions

Upgrade principles are identical: speed and capacity first, products before expansion, helpers when shelves go empty. Read our upgrades guide and tips page — they apply to both games.
