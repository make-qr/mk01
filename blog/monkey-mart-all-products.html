All Products in Monkey Mart — Unlock List

Every product in Monkey Mart has a production chain. Here is the full unlock list and recommended order.

Products in Monkey Mart unlock as you earn coins and progress. Each adds a new harvest-stock-sell loop. Unlock too many at once and you cannot keep shelves full. This list shows every major product and when to add it.

Starter Product

Bananas — available from the start. Your primary early income. Never let banana shelves go empty.

Early Game Products

  • Corn — first unlock; second revenue stream per customer
  • Eggs — after corn runs smoothly; needs chicken coop harvest

Mid Game Products

  • Milk — dairy station; pairs with bakery chain later
  • Flour — processed good; needs stable milk and egg supply

Late Game Products

  • Bread — high-value processed item; full production chain required
  • Snacks and packaged goods — highest per-customer revenue; assign dedicated helpers

Recommended Unlock Order

  1. Bananas (starter)
  2. Corn
  3. Eggs
  4. Milk
  5. Flour
  6. Bread and advanced goods

Only unlock the next product when the current one stays stocked during busy periods without your constant attention. Full upgrade timing: upgrades guide.
