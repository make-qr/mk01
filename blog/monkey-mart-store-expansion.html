Monkey Mart Store Expansion Guide

Expanding your Monkey Mart too early is the most common mid-game mistake. Here is when and how to grow your store the right way.

Store expansion in Monkey Mart unlocks new aisles, floors, and shelf space. More space means more products and more customers — but also more ground to cover. Expand before you have speed, capacity, and helpers ready and you will watch empty shelves drain your income.

When to Expand

Expand only when:

  • Speed is level 3+ and capacity is level 2+
  • At least one helper covers your main harvest or stock loop
  • Current shelves stay full during peak customer waves
  • You have spare coins after hiring without going broke

First Expansion Tips

Your first new aisle should host your second-best-selling product (usually corn). Place shelves along the shortest path from production to register. Do not spread products across the far end of a new floor unless a helper patrols that zone.

Multi-Floor Strategy

Each new floor adds vertical distance. Assign one helper per floor for stocking and consider a dedicated harvester for crops on that level. Upgrade speed again after each major expansion — more ground requires faster movement.

Layout Principles

  • High-demand products near entrance and checkout
  • Production patches as close to their shelves as possible
  • One product type per zone when helpers are assigned
  • Expand one section at a time, not the entire store at once

After Expansion Checklist

After buying new floor space: stock every new shelf before unlocking more products, hire or reassign a helper to the new zone, and play one full customer wave before spending on further upgrades.
