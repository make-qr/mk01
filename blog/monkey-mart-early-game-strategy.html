Monkey Mart Early Game Strategy for Fast Coins

Your first 20 minutes in Monkey Mart set the pace for everything after. This early game strategy maximizes coins per minute.

The Monkey Mart early game is about building a tight loop: harvest bananas, stock shelves, collect coins, buy speed. Every minute you leave shelves empty or skip register money, you fall behind the customer timer.

Minute 0–5: Master the Banana Loop

Do nothing except: pick bananas → stock banana shelf → collect payment → repeat. Buy speed 1 as soon as you can afford it. Do not expand, do not unlock corn yet.

Minute 5–10: Add Capacity

Buy capacity 1, then speed 2. You should now carry more bananas per trip and move noticeably faster. Banana shelves should rarely go empty.

Minute 10–15: Unlock Corn

Unlock corn and assign one shelf. Run alternating loops: bananas first (they fund everything), then corn. Buy capacity 2 when corn trips feel cramped.

Minute 15–20: Speed 3 and First Helper

Buy speed 3. If corn shelves empty while you harvest bananas, hire your first helper for whichever task you miss most. Read helper monkey timing.

Coin Per Minute Tips

  • Never stand idle — always be harvesting, stocking, or collecting
  • Collect register money every pass through the store
  • One upgrade per session goal keeps spending focused
  • Unlock eggs only when corn stays full for 2+ waves without you

What Not to Do Early

Avoid store expansion, decorative items, and third product lines until your first two products run with helper support. A small fast store earns more than a big empty one.
