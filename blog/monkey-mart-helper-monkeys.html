When to Hire Helper Monkeys in Monkey Mart

Helper monkeys automate harvesting, stocking, and checkout. Here is exactly when to hire your first one — and what job to give them.

Helper monkeys are Monkey Mart's automation system. Once hired, they repeat specific tasks so you can focus on bottlenecks elsewhere. Hiring too early wastes coins; hiring too late means lost sales from empty shelves.

When to Hire Your First Helper

Hire when ALL of these are true:

  • Speed and capacity are at level 2+
  • Corn is unlocked and selling regularly
  • Shelves go empty while you are harvesting
  • You have enough coins left after hiring to restock once

Best First Helper Job

Assign your first helper to whichever task you neglect most:

  • Harvester — if banana or corn fields sit ripe while you stock shelves
  • Stocker — if shelves empty while you are in the fields
  • Cashier support — only in late game when checkout queues form

Second and Third Helpers

Add a second helper when one automated worker cannot cover two product lines during peak traffic. The third helper usually appears in mid-to-late game when you have multiple floors and processed goods chains.

Common Hiring Mistakes

  • Hiring before speed upgrades — you still move too slowly between zones
  • Hiring multiple helpers at once — drains coins needed for restocking
  • Assigning helpers to low-traffic products instead of bestsellers

Helper + Upgrade Synergy

Helpers multiply the value of speed and capacity upgrades. A fast harvester with high capacity fills shelves faster than a slow one. Keep upgrading speed even after hiring staff.
