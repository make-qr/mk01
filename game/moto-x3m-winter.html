Moto X3M Winter - Ice-Cold Stunt Bike Racing

Moto X3M Winter sends your stunt bike across frozen landscapes — icy ramps, snow-covered loops, and slippery landings that punish bad timing. All 22 levels wrap the classic Moto X3M physics in a Christmas-winter skin with candy canes, snowmen, and frosty explosions. Lean into flips on frozen half-pipes and stick the landing before your rider shatters on the ice.

How to Play Moto X3M Winter

  • Throttle control: Up/W accelerates; ice reduces grip on landings
  • Air balance: Lean with A/D to rotate for frontflips and backflips
  • Watch slippery ramps: Winter surfaces slide longer — brake before sharp turns
  • Star ratings: Finish under par time with clean landings for 3 stars
  • Press R to restart: Winter obstacles are unforgiving — quick retries are essential

Moto X3M Winter Features

  • 22 winter-themed stunt tracks with holiday decorations
  • Icy physics that change how you approach landings
  • Full flip system for shaving seconds off your time
  • Part of the Moto X3M series — same controls, new scenery

Moto X3M Winter FAQ

Is Winter harder than the original Moto X3M?

Level difficulty is similar, but icy surfaces make landings trickier — expect more crashes early on.

How many levels does Winter have?

22 stunt levels, each rated 1–3 stars based on completion time.

Can I play Moto X3M Winter on mobile?

Yes. On-screen buttons mirror keyboard controls for gas, brake, and lean.
